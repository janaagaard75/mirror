## Summary

Mirror does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data — not to us, not to third parties, not anywhere.

## Camera

Mirror uses your device's front camera solely to display a live video feed on screen. The camera feed is never recorded, saved, analyzed, or sent off your device.

## No tracking

Mirror contains no analytics, no advertising, no crash reporting, and no third-party SDKs. It makes no network requests.

## Data collected

None.
